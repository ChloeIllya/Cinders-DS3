#------------------------------
# Changelog
#------------------------------

# Sidearms
- Cannon FP Cost: 500
- Church Cannon FP Cost: 500
- Hunter's Pistol FP Cost: 100
- Repeating Pistol FP Cost: 150
- Evelynn FP Cost: 200
- Hunter's Blunderbuss FP Cost: 150
- FP regen is now paused for 2 seconds after shooting.
- Fixed the behavior effects so they behave consistently. Previously they were applied multiple times.
- Reduced the Magic/Dark infusion FP/HP return for Cannons to 2%.
- Reduced the Magic/Dark infusion FP/HP return for Pistols to 1%.
- Added Fist of Gratia: deals heavy poise/stamina damage, but costs FP and stamina as the other sidearms do.

# Merchants
- Magnum Ursus now sells the Bear Set in his shop.
- Magnum Ursus's secret shop is now unlocked by presenting the Lordvessel, not the Blood of the Dark Soul.

# Treasure
- Lordvessel is found in the Flameless Shrine.

# Enemies
- Player-like AI now has an internal cooldown on WA moves (10 seconds), to prevent spam.
- Player-like AI will no longer hold spells.
- Player-like AI now has less stamina, which will reduce the length of their attack chains, which can be punishingly long otherwise.

# Bugfixes
- Fixed the alignment of the Repeating Pistol bullets.
- Adjusted Hunter's Blunderbuss bullet start angle to better fit the firing animation.
